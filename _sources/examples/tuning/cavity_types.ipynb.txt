    "# Tuning across cavity types\n",
    "\n",
    "Every cavity model declares **its own** tuning handles — there is no fixed list.\n",
    "The tuner asks the model what it can vary, so the same `cav.tune.run(...)` call\n",

    "wall radius, or a spline control point. Here we tune four different geometries to\n",
    "a target frequency and compare.\n",
    "\n",
    "See the [tuning guide](../../tuning)."
