    Note

    This section is a placeholder. Wakefield and impedance quantities are currently computed with the external time-domain solver ABCI; its numerical formulation is not documented here yet. See Wakefield Analysis for the interface and the normalised result schema, and the eigenmode impedance reconstruction (Eigenmode Analysis) for the equivalent-circuit spectrum that can be compared against a wakefield solve.
